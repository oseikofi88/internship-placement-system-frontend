Welcome To The College of Engineering Internship Placement System

This is the homepage of the College of Engineering Internship Placement System where 3rd year engineering students are required to sign up inorder to be placed into various companies for their vacation training exercise. Also various organizations who would like to give KNUST engineering students a chance to have their vacation training exercise in their companies should also sign up inorder to place an order on the number of students they need and the various departments they need the students from.
