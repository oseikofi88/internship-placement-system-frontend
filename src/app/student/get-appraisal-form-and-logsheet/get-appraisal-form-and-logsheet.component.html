Please Read The Following Instructions Carefully

1. You'll need to download both the logsheets and the confidential appraisal form.

2. For the log sheet make 8 copies, one for each week
(Please note that the required duration for the vacation training period is 8 weeks, if for some reason you can't do all the 8, Please contact your coordinator and let him be aware of the situation first.)

3. For each week, write down the activities you undertake during the course of the week and let your supervisor sign it at the end of the week.

4. Please make it a point to fill it as this is part of what your coordinators will use to grade you.

5.Immediately you get to know your supervisor in the company you are interning ,click on Update Supervisor Details button to fill a form with the fields
i)Your supervisor name
ii)Your supervisor email
iii)Your supervisor phone.

6. At the end of the day internship period, you will need to give the confidential appraisal form to your supervisor for him/her to sign and then seal it in an envelope for you.

7. You need to compile the list of all log sheets and and appraisal form along with your internship report and then submit them to your department when school reopens.

8. Concerning the report, every department has its own specification so you have to ask your department's internship coordinator on your department's specification for the report.
